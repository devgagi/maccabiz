About


The Maccabi North Association (the gymnastics school at Kibbutz Ma'ayan Zvi) was established in 2004 to provide a solution for gymnasts in Zichron Ya'acov and its surrounding area — from Pardes Hanna in the south to Haifa in the north and the Jezreel Valley in the east.


The purpose of the association is to teach children the basics of gymnastics, while the outstanding gymnasts join the National Israeli and Maccabi teams and represent the association in competitions and training camps in Israel and abroad.

Since its inception, Maccabi North has been one of the leading associations in the country in artistic gymnastics for boys and girls, and has raised outstanding gymnasts who have represented and continue to represent the State of Israel in European Championships, World Championships, Olympics and other international competitions.

The team of coaches and instructors educates the young athletes to love of people and their country, and cultivates physical and spiritual education.

The sports center is among the few in the country that have permanently-installed devices.
